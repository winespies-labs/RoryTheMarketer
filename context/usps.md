# Wine Spies: Brand USPs & Pain Points

## Core Value Proposition
Wine Spies finds hand-picked, high-quality wines from independent and small producers — wines you can't easily find elsewhere — and offers them to Operatives at the lowest price available, with the most flexible shipping solution in the business.

---

## The Pain Points We Solve

### Pain Point 1: Paying too much for mediocre, same-old wine
Most wine in supermarkets is controlled by a handful of large conglomerates running dozens of labels. The three-tier system (producer → distributor → retailer) means the same wines show up everywhere, diversity is low, and prices are inflated at every step.

**How we solve it:** We buy in large quantities, which gives us pricing leverage. We verify every offer against Wine-Searcher to confirm we have the lowest price available. If we can't beat the market price, we don't run the deal.

### Pain Point 2: Shipping wine is a nightmare
Wine is heavy. Shipping is expensive. It's legally restricted in many states. And someone has to be home to sign for it — which means missed deliveries, redelivery fees, and frustration.

**How we solve it:** The **Locker** feature lets customers check out with one or two bottles at a time and accumulate up to 12 bottles across multiple orders. When the locker hits 12 bottles, it ships free. Customers schedule the shipment on their terms — so they're actually home when it arrives.

### Pain Point 3: Great small producers are invisible
Small independent producers often can't afford the three-tier system. They don't have the volume for distributors, can't pay brokers or fund marketing, and never reach customers outside their local area.

**How we solve it:** We go direct. DTC online lets a small producer get wine into our hands and reach thousands of Operatives at once — wines they'd never find at their local shop or wine.com.

## The Three USPs

### USP 1: Best Price on Hand-Picked, High-Quality Wines
- We taste **every wine we sell** before it goes live
- We only sell wines that clear our quality bar
- We check Wine-Searcher to guarantee the lowest price
- If it doesn't meet both standards — quality AND price — it doesn't get an offer

**In copy:** Lead with the points and the % off. Make the price comparison real. Name the comparable bottles and what they cost.

### USP 2: The Locker — The Most Flexible Way to Build a Case
- Customers check out with 1–2 bottles at a time
- Bottles accumulate in their Locker across multiple orders
- At 12 bottles: ships free
- Customer schedules delivery — they're home when it arrives
- No more paying full shipping on every small order

**In copy:** The Locker is a feature worth explaining when relevant — especially for acquisition campaigns targeting new customers who've never heard of the model.

### USP 3: Satisfaction Guaranteed
- Every Operative should be happy with their purchase
- Customer service is a genuine differentiator — not a call center, not a chatbot
- We stand behind what we sell

**In copy:** Works best as a trust signal in acquisition emails, landing pages, and retargeting — lowers the barrier to a first purchase.

## What Wine Spies Is NOT
- Not a subscription club
- Not a mass-market discounter (the opposite)
- Not a marketplace with 1,000 options
- Not a wine education service
- Not the same as Wine.com, Vivino, or grocery store wine sections
